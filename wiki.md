# Seattle

![image](https://www.visittheusa.com/sites/default/files/styles/hero_l/public/images/hero_media_image/2022-04/5639157682e2053e7cfaddeea35c03a1.jpeg?h=1f16ef47&itok=fUdNyhRF)

Seattle is a city in the U.S. state of Washington, a coastal city with a pleasant climate. 
It is home to the **University of Washington**, one of the top 10 universities in the world. 
It is also called the * *Rainy City* * because it rains a lot.

## Space Needle
The Space Needle is a Seattle landmark that many visitors come to see every year, and you can go to the top to look down on the whole of Seattle. Every year on New Year's Eve, it also counts down to the fireworks.
- [Space Needle](https://www.spaceneedle.com/)

## Pike Place Market
Pike Place Market is a famous light spot in Seattle, with seafood, flowers, and food. The **first** Starbucks is also located here.
- [Pike Place Market](https://www.pikeplacemarket.org/)
